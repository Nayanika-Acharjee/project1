Shashi Paneer

The Best Recipe On Internet

Ingredients

  • Paneer-250g
  • Milk-1 cup
  • Paste of fried onion, ginger-garlic, green chili, cashew, raisins, few pieces of paneer.
  • Salt , Sugar and basic spices(jeera powder,coriander powder)and kasuri meeti powder

Instructions

  1. Put the paste in the hot oil and let it cook for somtime
  2. After the paste is perfectly cooked , put the spices on it
  3. Then add salt and again let it cooked for a few min
  4. Then put the milk in it< /li>
  5. add 3-4 tea spoon of sugar
  6. lastly garnish it with kasuri meeti, coriander and rose patels(optional)
